Understand

Individual Interviews

I made 5 interviews to get a deeper understanding of the users who use the app and found: People are struggling with login, navigation, forms, and projects. Also, there are 2 groups of people: Managers and clerks. With those insights, I selected the users and defined the scenarios for the usability tests.

Usability Testing

To uncover pain points while using the ios app, I conducted 5 usability test. I gave each user scenario based tasks to complete within the app:

  1. Imagine you are a manager and go on a business travel, but you have to report your expenses so the company gets your money back. A coworker told you about the mobile app Bizagi. Could you show me what you would do?
  2. Now that you have reported some expenses, as a manager, you need to approve expenses for your employees. You heard from a friend that you can do that too on Bizagi. What would you do?

Insights: Affinity Map

I synthesized my findings under 5 categories, the same I found in individual interviews. The findings from my research were then organized on an affinity map to better understand their relationship to each other and to determine priorities moving forward.

Base on the study, here are the 5 problems I decided to focus on:

  • Difficult to log in, too technical.
  • Difficult to send the activity of a case.
  • Challenge to navigate through the main features.
  • Difficult to switch between projects.
  • Difficult to know what is going on.
